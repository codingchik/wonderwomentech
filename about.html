Truth to Power was inspired by the more than 25% of enlisted military women who experience sexual trauma their service to our country. Truth to Power is a safe place to anonymously make your voice heard, share your journey of survival and join together ina community of support. Our name comes from a 1955 Quaker pamphlet about how love and peace stand firm in the face of violence and adversity.
